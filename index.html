Thanks to Real Sheet Collection you can simply play because you want to do it, whenever you want! And without depending on paper or dice.

Through the application it will be quick and easy to access all yours character sheets, and use when required the Dice Roller.

Tested for months by two different groups of RPG players with more than 10 years of experience!

  • If you are a player you will have with you everything you need to play.
  • If you are a dungeon master, on the other hand, you will have all the characters of the story with you, and thanks to the template saving system will also have the opportunity to create more of them instantly.
